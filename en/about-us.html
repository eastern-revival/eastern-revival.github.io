About Us

Initiative Introduction

"Eastern Revival" means the revival of the Eastern district of Hong Kong, returning the community to how it was before the pandemic.
This Initiative will hire professional tutors to teach young people affected by the epidemic a series of work skills. With young people’s vitality, they will carry out community care actions to bring love and needs into the community, empowering it.
Furthermore, young people will record community stories to show how they fought back against the pandemic and its ill effects. They will also work with healthcare professionals so they could help alleviate families in need. Together, this initiative aims to remind our community the important values in life and keep moving forward.

Initiative Goals

1) Improve young people's working skills through workshops.
2) Allow young people to gain work experiences through projects.
3) Create job opportunities by hiring workshop teachers who were affected by the pandemic.
4) Build positive outlook within our community by recording and telling stories and creating The Calendar.
