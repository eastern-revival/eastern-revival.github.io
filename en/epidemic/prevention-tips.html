Epidemic Prevention Tips

For kids

1. Avoid bringing your kids to gatherings.
2. If your child show symptoms of COVID-19 (fever, coughing), bring them to the hospital as soon as possible.

For adults

1. Keep at least 1.5m from each other in the office while eating or talking.
2. Periodically disinfect shared office items such as printers, keyboards, and microwaves.
3. Better yet, avoid sharing office items when possible.
4. Use 1:99 bleach to clean your home.
5. Exercise at least 30 minutes every day.

For the elderly

1. Cover your mouth when coughing or sneezing.
2. Eat food rich in vitamin-C (e.g. oranges, lemons, kiwis) and protein (e.g. bean products, meat, eggs).
3. Avoid leaving your house and ask others to bring groceries when possible.

Hong Kong, get better soon!

Symptoms of COVID-19 are similar to the common cold, including fever, exhaustion, dried throat, and difficult breathing.
COVID-19 spreads through saliva particles which enters through the mouth, nose, and eyes. It could also spread through excretion from the infected person.
Remember: Wash your hands, avoid touching your face, keep social distancing, cover your mouth when couching/sneezing. Do our part to save our community from this pandemic!
